About SaraHUD

SaraHUD is a custom HUD (Heads-Up Display) for Friday Night Funkin', created by Novikond. It provides a clean and modern interface for players to enhance their gaming experience.

Features:

  • Artistic design
  • Customizable elements
  • Improved readability
  • Compatibility with various mods

SaraHUD is a popular choice among FNF players who appreciate a sleek and functional HUD. Its clean aesthetics and user-friendly features make it a great addition to any player's setup.
